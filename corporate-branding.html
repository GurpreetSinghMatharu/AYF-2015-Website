Benefits to Sponsors

Sponsoring Amity Youth Festival 2015 (AYF’15) presents a unique opportunity for the industry to highlight their commitment and support to the youth, the future of the country.
Sponsorship is a powerful way to reinforce corporate image and promoting company’s brand/s to a targeted audience.
Highly visible and cost-effective sponsorship of AYF’15 gives you the opportunity to develop an association between your company and world class events, foster goodwill with young customers and strengthen your involvement with the social & cultural life of the country.
The Festival is seen as synonymous with quality at the highest level with an appeal to the young demographic groups.
Amity Youth Festival reaches out to the upper echelon of young professionals and draws patronage from a diverse population of students, alumni, and consumers between the ages of 15-65;
AYF generates substantial media interest as seen during AYF’12, AYF’13; & AYF’14
The Festival produces and distributes large number of promotional brochures, posters, banners and programmes;
A growing number of National & International journalists visit the Festival each year.


General Sponsorship benefits include:

  • extensive branding/marketing opportunities and exposure during
  • the entire life of the event;
  • invitation letters will be sent to more than 500 Universities &
  • Colleges in India and more than 200 Universities & Colleges overseas;
  • access to influential and engaged delegates;
  • the opportunity to unlock and share knowledge;
  • places the Sponsor Company at the heart of the festival that
  • dominates the University Campus which is known by the entire student body and directly experienced by over 50,000 students;
  • day of event and post event recognition;
  • photo opportunities with the event participants and prize winners.
  • ability to distribute promotional materials at the festival(50,000 students, faculty, alumni and professionals will be in attendance) ;
  • VIP seating at events.

General Definitions

  • Sponsor: Refers to the organization or individual contributing money/funds or in-kind contributions in exchange for specific benefits.
  • Sponsee: The organization receiving money/funds or in-kind contributions in return for delivering specific benefits.
  • Presenting Sponsor: For any mention of the event, the sponsor has its name placed just below (or above) the sponsored property, connected by the phrase presented by, presents, or similar constructions.
  • Title Sponsor: For any mention of the Property, the name of the sponsor is incorporated into the name of the sponsored property.

PROHIBITED SPONSORSHIP

In judging the suitability of a sponsor and/or sponsorship message, Amity University is guided by the following. The Sponsor is not involved in advertising or promoting:
  • Alcohol
  • Tobacco Products
  • Firearms/Weapons
  • Fireworks
  • Pornography
  • Political Cause
  • Social Cause
  • Religious Cause
  • Competitors
  • Comparative Advertising
