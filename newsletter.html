Amity Youth Fest 2014 Newsletter




Day 1 Events 06/02/2014

Sparkle and Spice

Amity School of Hospitality showcased talent, skills and mouth-watering delicacies in Sparkle & Spice Mock-tail Competition at Main Ground on the first day of Amity Youth Fest 2014. Seven teams participated with two members in each team and our Honourable AVC graced the event. The mock-tails were a treat to the eyes, resplendent in their colours and presentation. The teams had to prepare two mocktails one of their own choice and second according to the theme which was Youth.


Robowars

Robowars Competition organised by EEE and EIE Department. Twenty Teams participated in this competition. Mr. Kamlesh Pandey judged the event. The winner were awarded with the trophies and the certificates. This event was a sneak peak into the future of how wars/ fights would be fought. Participants looked cheering and enthusiastic.



Antaragini

“Ho Kahin bhi aag lekin aag jalni chahiye”-the essence of Nukkad Natak –Antaragini harped upon socio-cultural issues with a call for positive action.

Rhetorical Square

Rhetorical Square- the debate competition of AICC & AIESR on the topic “Spirituality v/s Science” exhibited the eloquence and oratory skills of the participants. ABP News Anchor and talented journalist Ms. Sumaira Khan enthused the participants with her presence.

Gaming Corner

Console Gaming Organised by AITEM was a treat for the game enthusiasts. Around twenty people participated.


Envisage

The competition was held by Amity Institute of Biotechnology in which participants had to make models related to physical and biological sciences using scrap. This event was a loud proclamation the necessity of recycling. Nine Teams participated in this competition and it was judged by Mr. K.C. Upadhyaya, Advisor of AIB.


Fasion Show

The glitz and glamour quotient recieved an impetus with the fashion show. Fashionistas with their sassy and suave style captured the fantasy of all.


Aphesia

Student Bands across NCR were pitted against each other in this musical extravaganza. The seven notes of music created a symphony which reached a cresendo and made the spectators euphoric.


Day 2 Events 07/02/2014

AYF’14 has entered its second day, offering the participants more and more opportunities to explore their talents. The proctorial board of the university and the security personnel are doing a commendable job in ensuring that no untoward event happens during the fest.



Sparkling Spring

ASH organized “Sparkling Spring” to give wings to the creativity of participants in arranging flowers


Track The Pack

Amity Institute of Information Technology(AIIT) has organized an event “Track The Pack” on 7 Feb 2014 where the objective is to locate the packet while traversing from one point to another using defined topology. This event consists of 2 member team, total participating team was 7 explores the concept of networking things dynamic skills and expertise. Teams participated in the event from AIIT. The winners are awarded with trophies and certificates.


Lan Gaming

Amity Institute of Information Technology(AIIT) has organized Lan Gaming event on 6 Feb 2014. 10 teams participated in the event from Delhi University, Jayppee, Jamia,and other colleges of NCR. Each team has 5 members. The winners will be awarded with trophies and certificates.


Youth Parliament

“Youth Parliament” of AISS provided a platform for the youth to speak up and make an impact on issues of societal concern


Nritya Arsh

Classical rendition of varied dance forms through exquisite rhythmic movements paid a tribute to the rich cultural heritage of our country.


Crime Scene Investigation

The thrill of solving the crime using analytical skills put the participants as well as the audience on tenterhooks. AIFS takes credit for this unique show.


Broadsway

The theatre show had college across NCR vying for the top slot with shows on social and cultural issues


Sahitya Manthan

the Literary Quiz of AIESR & AICC showcased the literary competency of the participating teams. The literary feast tickled the literary buds of the participants and the audience.


Carnival

Carnival by ASFL has coalesced cultures from across the world true to the mission and vision to bring world to Amity and Amity to the world.


Fun with Mooting

The aim of the event was to come up with visionary ideas to conserve and preserve the environment. The intention was to pitch these ideas in front of the jury. Different colleges participated in this event and made it successful. The ideas that had hurdles in implementation were eliminated and Ideas could be explained either by a working model or by a presentation has been selected for the final round.
